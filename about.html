I am a Junior Web Developer

I am a Junior Web Developer in Calgary, Alberta. My interest in web development sparked through designing websites for local businesses utilizing web template websites, such as Wix and Shopify. After which, I enrolled in the Web Development program at SAIT…and the rest is history.

I believe web development is an art: the viewport is a blank canvas awaiting to be painted with colorful code and molded through logic-based creativity. I have a passion for building innovative, marketable websites that include intuitive features and attractive eye candy to enhance the UX for local businesses.

We are not defined by what we have done, but what we dream of accomplishing. I am fuelled by learning new coding superpowers to style engaging user-friendly designs and aspire to continually grow and learn new coding talents. To me, web developing is not a job, it’s a passion.
